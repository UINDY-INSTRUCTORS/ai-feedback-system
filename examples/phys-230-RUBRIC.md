# PHYS/MENG 230 - Lab Report Rubric

**Course**: PHYS/MENG 230  
**Assignment**: Lab Report  
**Type**: lab  
**Total Points**: 100

---

## Criterion 1: Abstract & Description (10%)

Abstract clearly and concisely states the project idea, results, and precision.
Description explains the significance of the project.

### Performance Levels

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Excellent** | 7-10 | Abstract is clear and concise with precise results. Description clearly conveys significance. |
| **Good** | 4-6 | Abstract present with basic merit. Description gives some idea of significance. |
| **Poor** | 0-3 | Abstract missing or lacks substantial merit. Description missing or unclear. |

### Excellent Indicators
- Project idea stated absolutely clearly
- Results stated with precision/uncertainty
- Significance of project well explained

### Good Indicators
- Basic idea of project is stated
- Result is stated (may lack precision)
- Description present but could be clearer

### Poor Indicators
- Project idea not clearly stated
- Result missing or stated unclearly
- Description missing or doesn't convey significance

### Keywords
abstract, description, significance, result, precision, purpose

### Common Issues
- Abstract too vague or overly detailed
- Results stated without precision or uncertainty
- Significance of project not explained
- Missing connection to course concepts

---

## Criterion 2: Circuit Schematic, Background and Discussion (20%)

Important circuit details are described clearly and correctly. For open-ended
projects, work shows originality and interest.

### Performance Levels

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Excellent** | 13-20 | All important circuit details described perfectly. Highly original and interesting. |
| **Good** | 7-12 | Most important details described clearly. Fairly original with some interest. |
| **Poor** | 0-6 | Important circuit details missing or incorrect. Little originality or interest. |

### Excellent Indicators
- All important circuit details present and clear
- Presentation is perfectly clear and correct
- Open-ended projects: completely new and original
- Rich and interesting content

### Good Indicators
- Most important details present
- Generally clear and correct presentation
- Open-ended projects: fairly original
- Has at least some interest

### Poor Indicators
- Important details missing or incorrect
- Presentation unclear or wrong
- Open-ended projects: not original
- Little interest or significance

### Keywords
circuit, schematic, background, diagram, component, design, original

### Common Issues
- Component values not labeled on schematic
- No explanation of how circuit works
- Missing pin numbers or IC part numbers
- Schematic not readable or poorly drawn
- No discussion of design choices

---

## Criterion 3: Circuit Function (40%)

Circuit is constructed and functions correctly. Schematic and code are readable
with documentation. Correct operation validated through measurements and statistics.

### Performance Levels

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Excellent** | 26-40 | Circuit functions flawlessly. Schematic/code are excellent. Function quantitatively validated. |
| **Good** | 14-25 | Circuit functions almost perfectly. Schematic/code fairly readable. Some validation attempted. |
| **Poor** | 0-13 | Circuit not constructed or not functional. No demonstration of correct operation. |

### Excellent Indicators
- Circuit functions flawlessly
- Schematic and code are works of art - easy to read
- Sufficient embedded documentation
- Function validated quantitatively with measurements
- Statistical analysis used to validate operation

### Good Indicators
- Circuit all there and functions almost perfectly
- Schematic/code fairly easy to read
- Some documentation embedded
- Some attempt at validation with measurements
- Basic statistical analysis present

### Poor Indicators
- Circuit not constructed or not functional
- No demonstration of correct operation
- Schematic/code difficult to understand
- Minimal or no documentation
- No validation measurements

### Keywords
circuit, function, operation, test, measurement, validate, code, schematic

### Common Issues
- No photos showing physical circuit construction
- Circuit built but not tested/validated
- No code comments or documentation
- Missing test measurements to prove functionality
- Statistical analysis not performed
- Code works but is unreadable

## Criterion 4: Results (including stats exercises) (20%)

Results are complete with correct units and formulas. Valid interpretation showing
understanding. Clear graphical representations with labels. Statistical exercises correct.

### Performance Levels

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Excellent** | 13-20 | Complete results with perfect interpretation. Clear graphs with labels. All stats exercises correct. |
| **Good** | 7-12 | Mostly complete results with reasonable interpretation. Graphs fairly clear. Most stats correct. |
| **Poor** | 0-6 | Results missing or incomplete. Invalid interpretation. Graphs missing or unclear. Few stats correct. |

### Excellent Indicators
- Results present and complete
- Units clearly given and correct
- Perfect understanding of physical system and techniques
- Clear, understandable graphs with axis labels
- Formulas provided where appropriate
- All statistical exercises present and correct

### Good Indicators
- Results present and mostly complete
- Units given and correct
- Shows significant understanding
- Graphs fairly clear and understandable
- Most statistical exercises correct

### Poor Indicators
- Results missing or seriously incomplete
- No interpretation or invalid interpretation
- Units incorrect or missing
- Graphs missing or indecipherable
- No formulas or incorrect formulas
- Few or no statistical exercises correct

### Keywords
results, data, graph, plot, units, statistics, mean, standard deviation, uncertainty

### Common Issues
- Missing axis labels or units on graphs
- Results stated without units
- No interpretation or discussion of results
- Graphs present but not referenced in text
- Statistical exercises incomplete or incorrect
- No comparison to theoretical predictions
- Results not formatted in tables where appropriate

---

## Criterion 5: Conclusion (10%)

Conclusion is present and complete, with final result clearly stated and
uncertainty estimates included.

### Performance Levels

| Level | Points | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Excellent** | 7-10 | Complete conclusion with clear final result and uncertainty estimates. |
| **Good** | 4-6 | Conclusion present and reasonably complete. Final result stated clearly. |
| **Poor** | 0-3 | Conclusion missing or seriously incomplete. |

### Excellent Indicators
- Conclusion present and complete
- Final result stated clearly
- Uncertainty estimates included
- Results connected to objectives

### Good Indicators
- Conclusion present and reasonably complete
- Final result stated clearly
- May lack uncertainty discussion

### Poor Indicators
- Conclusion missing or very brief
- Final result not stated clearly
- No connection to project goals

### Keywords
conclusion, result, summary, uncertainty, estimate, findings

### Common Issues
- Conclusion just repeats results without interpretation
- No uncertainty or error discussion
- Doesn't connect back to objectives
- Missing or extremely brief
- No reflection on what was learned
